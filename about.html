Overview

Has it ever happened that you savored the munchies in the party that you last attended? Or the food in the wedding uplifted the gastronomic ambience? It was apparently because the event was catered. When there is an upcoming event, details like how many guests are to be fed, what is to be served, how the entire process of cooking, serving and cleaning is to be carried out with professionalism and precision becomes a mammoth task and that is when you require catering services.

How We Work


We hold an elaborate meeting and work very closely with our clients and discuss staffing, scheduling, and craft a versatile menu. After agreeing upon the details, we give them a draft contract for review. We try to make this process as hassle free as possible .We have no hidden charges and explain the cost incurred very well.

On the day of the event, we set up the venue with the captain supervising and synchronizing the staffs, keeping the deadline in mind. We accommodate the requirements of all the guests.
